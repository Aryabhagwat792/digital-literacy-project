Social Media Do's and Don'ts


DO’S
1.
	Keep your profile settings private
	2.
	Think before you post , will this embarrass the interviewers
	3.
	Share all achievementsLike hackathon , projects , certificates
	4.
	To build credibility use real photo on LinkedIn
	5.
	Verify sources used to avoid spreading misinformation
	6.
	Enable 2FA authentication
	7.
	Use strong passwords
	

DON’TS
1.
	Make sure to have consent other people before posting their photos and videos
	2.
	Do not share OTP’s publicly
	3.
	Do not share content which has bullying any individual or group
	4.
	Do not click on unverified or unknown links
	5.
	Do not use someone’s image without crediting them
	6.
	Do not post in someone’s influence that would make you to regret
	7.
	Use verified apps to share your personal information
