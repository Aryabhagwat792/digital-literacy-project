Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness


PART B — STAY SAFE ONLINE: Prevention Checklist for College Students


General Online Safety


         Tip          ,            What to do
1.
	Use strong password
	Use 18+ characters. Use a free password manager to save all passwords
	2.
	Enable 2FA
	Have 2FA on Gmail ,LinkedIn ,GitHub , UPI app
	3.
	Do not click or use unverified link or account
	Never click links in SMS, WhatsApp, or email from unknown senders 
	4.
	Use latest updates
	Install latest app updates 
	5.
	Be cautious while using public wi-fi
	Avoid using UPI, logging in bank accounts using public wifi
	6.
	Go through all app permissions before using it
	Regularly check which apps have access to your camera, microphone, contacts
	

Financial / UPI Safety 


        Tip                 What to do
1.
	Check bank SMS alerts immediately as well as regularly
	If you see an unexpected debit alert of money withdrawl , call your bank's 24x7 helpline within the hour. 
	2.
	Set daily UPI transaction limits
	In your bank or UPI app settings, set a daily transaction cap. 
	3.
	CRITICAL  Scanning QR
	Make sure to verify the QR before using it
	4.
	Never share your OTP or PIN with anyone.
	Ignore, block, and report the caller immediately.
